About

Banaras Hindu University (BHU), located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, is a prestigious public central university established in 1916 by Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya, with support from notable figures like Dr. Annie Besant. Renowned for its expansive 1,370-acre campus, BHU is Asia's largest residential university, accommodating over 30,000 students. The university comprises six institutes and 14 faculties, offering a wide range of disciplines. As of January 2025, the Vice-Chancellor is Prof. Sudhir K. Jain, and the Chancellor is Giridhar Malaviya.
